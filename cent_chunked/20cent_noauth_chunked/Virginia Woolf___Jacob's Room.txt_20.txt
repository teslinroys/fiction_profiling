A harsh
and unhappy voice cried something unintelligible. And then suddenly all
the leaves seemed to raise themselves. "Jacob! Jacob!" cried Bonamy, standing by the window. The leaves sank
down again. "Such confusion everywhere!" exclaimed Betty Flanders, bursting open the
bedroom door. Bonamy turned away from the window. "What am I to do with these, Mr. Bonamy?" She held out a pair of Jacob's old shoes.

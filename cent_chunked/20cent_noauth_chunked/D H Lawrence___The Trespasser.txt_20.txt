'That is better,' he said. 'I was afraid of the pins,' said she. 'I've been dodging them for the last hour,' he said, laughing, as she
put her arms under his coat again for warmth. She laughed, and, making a small, moaning noise, as if of weariness and
helplessness, she sank her head on his chest. He put down his cheek
against hers. 'I want rest and warmth,' she said, in her dull tones. 'All right!' he murmured.
